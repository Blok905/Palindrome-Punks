Art-Drop Roulette

Game Overview

This game combines aspects of holder incentives, randomized luck and exciting community engagement!

Spin the wheel of holder names - Only one victor will emerge

Feeling lucky, punk?


Rules

  • 1 Punk = 1 Ticket
  • Holding multiple punks improves your odds
  • Discord required for participation
  • Exclusive for Palindrome Punk holders

Prizes

The rewards revolve around high resolution pixel art inscribed on exotic sats.

One of two rewards are possible for participants:

  1. Inscription air-drop

    2. OR (if successfully auctioned)

  2. $BTC proceeds from auction split amongst holders
